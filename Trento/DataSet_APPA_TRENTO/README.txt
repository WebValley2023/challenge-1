Trentino Datasets

These are three different datasets from APPA air monitoring stations

Each dataset has a different timeframe and contains data from 8 different stations in Trentino.

These datasets exclusively contain information about pollutants.

DATASETS 
1) 2013 to 2023
2) 2016 to 2020(Februray)
3) 2020(March) to 2022(June)
